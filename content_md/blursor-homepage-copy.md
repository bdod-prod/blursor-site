## Bridge

Neither are most practitioners.

There's a real gap between what researchers are finding out about how AI systems see, retrieve, and cite content — and what the people managing that content actually know. It's not a motivation problem. It's a volume problem. The research doesn't stop publishing because you have a site to run.

BLURSOR exists in that gap.

---

## How it works

We monitor the research. We filter for what's relevant to visibility, citation, and how generative engines make decisions about content. We read the papers. We extract the findings that have practical implications and strip out what doesn't. Then we publish — the insight, the supporting data, and what it means for your content.

No paraphrased abstracts. No takes dressed up as research. The paper is always linked.
