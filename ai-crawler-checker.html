Why this matters

AI reads your site differently than people do

When someone asks ChatGPT, Google's AI, or Perplexity for a recommendation, those tools read websites to decide what to suggest. But most of them read your site like a plain text document — they don't run the code that makes a page look finished in a browser.

So if your content only appears after the page "loads," AI can be left staring at a near-empty page. And a page AI can't read is a page it won't recommend. This tool fetches your site the same way AI does and shows you, in plain sight, exactly what it gets — so you know where you stand.
