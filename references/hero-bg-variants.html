How to use: copy the SVG block for your chosen variant and paste it as the first child of <div class="article-header">, before <div class="layout-grid">.

The .article-header already has position: relative; overflow: hidden and .article-header__bg has position: absolute; inset: 0; pointer-events: none — the SVG inherits these.

Tip: assign variants to articles based on topic feel — arcs from left for analytical/diagnostic pieces, diagonal lines for trend/pattern pieces, horizon arcs for broad survey papers, two-point tension for comparative research.
